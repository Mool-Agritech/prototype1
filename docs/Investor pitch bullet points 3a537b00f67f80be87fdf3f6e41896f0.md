## 1) What sets us apart from competitors?

- **Practice-linked pricing, not practice-agnostic cover.** Every existing player (Kshema, Pula, IBISA, InRisk) prices climate risk alone. Mool ties premium and payout to *verified agroecology adoption* — the only one selling resilience-building, not just harm-reduction.[[1]](https://www.notion.so/6b1e55b2a3c04efaa33d92785a7933cd)[[2]](https://www.notion.so/39f37b00f67f80a685d6c085551579ef)
- **Plot-level triggers vs. block-level basis risk.** PMFBY settles at block level (~0.3 correlation); Mool targets plot-level data (0.6–0.8 achievable) — directly attacking the basis-risk gap that left ~60% of drought-hit Vidarbha farmers unpaid in 2023–24.[[1]](https://www.notion.so/6b1e55b2a3c04efaa33d92785a7933cd)
- **We serve who others exclude.** Tenant/landless smallholders with no land title or credit history — structurally shut out of PMFBY (serves farm owners) and formal lending.[[1]](https://www.notion.so/6b1e55b2a3c04efaa33d92785a7933cd)
- **Orchestrator, not risk-holder.** Mool bundles agroecology inputs + parametric cover + training and sits as the coordinating layer — competitors either own the insurer license (Kshema) or sell only data (SatSure).[[1]](https://www.notion.so/6b1e55b2a3c04efaa33d92785a7933cd)
- **Distribution + trust via women-led SHGs**, framed as a family well-being program — a trust layer competitors selling top-down to insurers/governments don't have.[[1]](https://www.notion.so/6b1e55b2a3c04efaa33d92785a7933cd)

## 2) Why aren't they already doing this?

- **They sit in a different part of the stack.** InRisk and SatSure sell *upstream* to insurers/reinsurers/banks; they don't own smallholder distribution. Kshema owns the insurer layer but is practice-agnostic. None combine practice-linking + embedded distribution.[[2]](https://www.notion.so/39f37b00f67f80a685d6c085551579ef)[[1]](https://www.notion.so/6b1e55b2a3c04efaa33d92785a7933cd)
- **Practice-linking is operationally hard.** It requires an on-ground verification spine (satellite + IoT + geo-fenced photos + QR) *and* a community mobilization layer — expensive, slow, low-margin work that pure insurtech/data firms avoid.[[1]](https://www.notion.so/6b1e55b2a3c04efaa33d92785a7933cd)
- **Incumbents o**ptimize for the insurer buyer, not the farmer.[[2]](https://www.notion.so/39f37b00f67f80a685d6c085551579ef)
- **The B2C-to-farmer model is a known trap** — thin margins, language/trust barriers, high distress. Peers (Pula, IBISA) all retreated to selling into vested partners; few will do the harder community + agroecology build Mool is built around.[[3]](https://www.notion.so/3a037b00f67f80a7936ce0c8149202e7)[[2]](https://www.notion.so/39f37b00f67f80a685d6c085551579ef)
- **Tenant/landless segment is unattractive to incumbents** — no land documentation, no easy KYC — so it's left underserved despite being the acute-need core.[[1]](https://www.notion.so/6b1e55b2a3c04efaa33d92785a7933cd)

## 4) Technology-wise, what is the innovation?

- **Plot-level parametric trigger engine** using 4–5 satellite + weather sources + ecosystem data, with region-specific features mapped to actual farmer pain points
- **Practice-linked risk & pricing model** — a composite crop-stress index (drought/flood/heat sub-scores from VHI, dry-spell days, rainfall, soil moisture, LST/GDD anomalies) feeding a resilience-tiered premium.
- **Dual ML models:** (a) *insurance side* —  predicting seasonal anomaly; (b) *credit side* — financial distress/default from climate + coverage features.[[4]](https://www.notion.so/3a537b00f67f8093a67ed5b7769816f9)
- **Verification & MRV data spine** — satellite + IoT + geo-fenced, time-stamped photos + QR traceability. Double duty: triggers payouts *and* feeds pricing, data products, and carbon MRV.[[1]](https://www.notion.so/6b1e55b2a3c04efaa33d92785a7933cd)
- **Fast payout rails** — plot-level triggers → UPI payout targeted at 1 min–72 hrs (aligns with PMFBY's new 72-hr loss-reporting mandate).[[1]](https://www.notion.so/6b1e55b2a3c04efaa33d92785a7933cd)[[5]](https://www.notion.so/a62e866d9772432b9e5543173fe4b69a)
- **Farmer interface concept** — multilingual, voice-enabled WhatsApp chatbot
- *Backtest evidence (PoC):* Vidarbha, Taluka level, 2020–2024, Kharif primary, six distressed districts, PMFBY as baseline — quantifying basis-risk reduction.[[1]](https://www.notion.so/6b1e55b2a3c04efaa33d92785a7933cd)

## 5) How we plan to start & grow ⭐ *(highest weight)*

**Three-phase model — the through-line is the funding curve: institutions cover a rising share of premium (partial → 100%) as farmers move toward debt-free.**[[1]](https://www.notion.so/6b1e55b2a3c04efaa33d92785a7933cd)

- **Phase 1 — Prove (beachhead).**
    - Blended institutional stack (DFIs, state schemes, FPOs) funds premiums; farmers pay little to nothing.[[1]](https://www.notion.so/6b1e55b2a3c04efaa33d92785a7933cd)
    - Stand up women-led SHG distribution; onboard farmers on ~10% of land (the "10% Rule" to cap downside); agroecology training; deploy verification spine; launch practice-linked parametric product.[[1]](https://www.notion.so/6b1e55b2a3c04efaa33d92785a7933cd)
    - Revenue: thin insurance margin + early data subscriptions + input margin.[[1]](https://www.notion.so/6b1e55b2a3c04efaa33d92785a7933cd)
    - Beachhead market: ~100K acres (~$3.2M), Insurer + FPO clusters.[[1]](https://www.notion.so/6b1e55b2a3c04efaa33d92785a7933cd)
- **Phase 2 — Scale.**
    - Institutional stack funds the majority of premium; commercial financing enters as outcomes verify.[[1]](https://www.notion.so/6b1e55b2a3c04efaa33d92785a7933cd)
    - Multi-district expansion; input & equipment micro-enterprises; FPO-routed market access + certified offtake; data subscriptions grow; early carbon pipeline.[[1]](https://www.notion.so/6b1e55b2a3c04efaa33d92785a7933cd)
    - Addressable: 50–70M acres (~$2.2B).[[1]](https://www.notion.so/6b1e55b2a3c04efaa33d92785a7933cd)
- **Phase 3 — Future-proof.**
    - 100% of premium institution-funded (village-level foundations); farmers effectively debt-free.[[1]](https://www.notion.so/6b1e55b2a3c04efaa33d92785a7933cd)
    - Mature agroecology + deep MRV; primary commercial lines shift to data subscription, export-compliant offtake, carbon/PES, advisory.[[1]](https://www.notion.so/6b1e55b2a3c04efaa33d92785a7933cd)
